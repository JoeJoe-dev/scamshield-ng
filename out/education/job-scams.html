Job Scams

Fake recruitment agencies and pay-to-work schemes are rampant in Nigeria, especially targeting fresh graduates.

Prevention Tips

Never pay any fee to get a job — legitimate employers do not charge application fees.

Verify the company on the CAC portal before attending any interview.

Official companies use company email domains, not Gmail or Yahoo.

If asked to meet at a remote location, decline and report.

Warning Signs

⚠ Upfront payment required
⚠ No verifiable company address
⚠ Job offer without interview
⚠ Salary too high for the role
